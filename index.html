Biography

Forhad is a graduate student who loves to work on problem solving projects and project based research topics. He worked on IoT and Machine Learning projects under the supervision of Dr. M Rokonuzzaman at National University, Bangladesh. Additionally, he worked on OpenCV based Fire Rescue Navigator System under the supervision of Shoma Khanom, Head of the Department, Department of ECE, National University, Bangladesh. In addition, he was an instructor in Programming Department at GURUKUL. On top of that, he worked as a Software Developer at Everbest Informatics LTD. Currently Forhad is studying MSc in Software Engineering at the University of Westminster.

His research interests include Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Internet of Things, Computer Vision, Robotics, Robotic Process Automation, Electronics, Generative Models, Data Science, Mobile and Web App Development.

Forhad enjoys traveling around the world which can enable new opportunities to accumulate knowledge. He has a knack for good music as well. Learning something new is one of the most cherished hobbies of Forhad.
